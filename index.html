About the Student Election

Election Overview

Welcome to the Student Council Elections for the academic year 2023-2024. This platform allows students to participate in a democratic process to elect their representatives for various positions in the Student Council.

The elected council members will represent the student body in various academic and administrative matters, organize campus events, and work towards improving student life on campus.

Important Dates

Nomination Period: June 1 - June 10, 2023

Campaign Period: June 11 - June 20, 2023

Voting Period: June 21 - June 23, 2023

Results Announcement: June 24, 2023

Election Rules & Guidelines

Eligibility Criteria

All candidates must be currently enrolled students with a minimum GPA of 2.5.
Candidates must not have any pending disciplinary actions against them.
Candidates must be available for the entire academic year.
Only students who have completed at least one semester at the university are eligible to contest.

Campaign Rules

Campaigning is only allowed during the designated campaign period.
No defamatory or offensive content is allowed in campaign materials.
Campaign expenses must not exceed $200, and receipts must be submitted to the Election Committee.
No campaigning is allowed within 50 feet of voting stations on election days.
Use of university resources for campaign purposes must be pre-approved by the Election Committee.

Voting Rules

Each student is entitled to one vote per position.
Students must present their valid student ID to vote.
Proxy voting is not allowed under any circumstances.
Any attempt to manipulate the voting process will result in disqualification and disciplinary action.

Election Code of Conduct

All candidates and their supporters must maintain decorum and respect throughout the election process.
Any complaints regarding violations must be submitted in writing to the Election Committee within 24 hours of the incident.
The decision of the Election Committee in all matters related to the election shall be final.
